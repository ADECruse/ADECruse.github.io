A Zero Waste Index for Bristol City

This website's goal is to provide a searchable index of food products that have zero or minimal packaging and where they can be found for sale in the city of Bristol, UK.

Based on the Zero Waste philosophy, which you can read about here, my friend wanted an easier way to shop according to this philosophy in Bristol. So he asked me to help him to design and build this website.
